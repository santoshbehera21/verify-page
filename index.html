How to Use This SIP Calculator

  1. Enter Monthly Investment: Input the amount you plan to invest every month (minimum ₹500).
  2. Set Investment Period: Adjust the slider to select how many years you want to continue the SIP.
  3. Expected Return Rate: Set the annual return rate you expect from your investment (typically 12% for equity funds).
  4. Calculate: Click the calculate button to see your projected returns.
  5. Analyze Results: View your estimated maturity amount, total investment, and wealth gained.

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on compound interest formula. Actual returns may vary based on market conditions.
